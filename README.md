# 🦭 Happy Birthday, Yilin! 🎉

A little seal-themed birthday website with a built-in mini-game.

**Live site:** https://leearthur56.github.io/yilin-birthday/

## What's inside

- **`index.html`** — a cozy ocean birthday landing page with bobbing seals, drifting
  bubbles, confetti, and a "Make a Wish" button.
- **`game.html` + `game.js`** — *Seal & Bunny Birthday Catch*, a small canvas arcade game:
  bunnies 🐰 toss birthday treats off an iceberg and you slide your seal 🦭 to catch
  fish 🐟, cake 🎂, hearts 💖, gifts 🎁 and stars ⭐ — while dodging the pufferfish 🐡.

## How to play

- **Move:** arrow keys / `A` `D`, or just move your mouse (drag on touch screens).
- Catch treats to score. Consecutive catches build a **combo multiplier** for bonus points.
- Catching a **pufferfish 🐡** costs a life — you have three ★.
- Your best score is saved locally in your browser.

## Run it locally

It's plain static HTML/CSS/JS — no build step. Just open `index.html`, or serve the folder:

```bash
python -m http.server 8000
# then visit http://localhost:8000
```
